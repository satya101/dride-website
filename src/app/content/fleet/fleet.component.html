drideFleet

Managing a fleet is tough. Although fleet management software exists,
Dride offers a new layer of interaction with the car and driver - a hardware & software solution that leverages computer vision to help you keep your fleet under control. Telematics boxes may be what you're used to, but Dride is what you need.

Real time view

Dride is able to track your fleet vehicles in real time, allowing you to see where the vehicle is and even follow a live stream from the car’s camera. Safety alerts are updated on the dashboard instantly.

Driver Profile

By using computer vision algorithms, Dride can profile drivers and monitor their driving. We can alert you about speeding, distance keeping, and lane departure. This allows you to see how your drivers use their cars.

Tailored To Your Fleet

drideOS, the operation system that powers our devices, is both open-source and well-documented, so building custom features is a breeze. We offer a vast array of extensions, so if you need a custom feature or integration, let’s talk!

Security & Privacy

We've implemented the industry’s most stringent security standards in order to keep your fleet's data safe and private.
