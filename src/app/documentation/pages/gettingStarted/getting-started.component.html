How to build your own Dride

A Raspberry Pi-based connected dashcam

Hello makers, and thank you for taking the time to build a Dride! We’re excited to see what you do with Dride and are looking forward to your feedback! Let's get started.

What you will need:

  • Raspberry Pi Zero W or Raspberry Pi 3
  • Pi Camera
  • 3m micro USB cable
  • Dride-Case & Dride-HAT

Getting started:

First you need to burn your SD card with the latest version of drideOS. The best way to create the SD card is by using Dride Flasher


Download Dride Flasher

Dride Flasher

macOS

Dride Flasher

Windows (x64)
  • Download Dride Flasher
  • Connect an SD card reader with the SD card inside.
  • Open Dride Flasher and select the SD card to which you want to write your image as well as the OS type. Review your selections and click 'Flash!' to begin writing data to the SD card.

Control the device

Download Dride's app for iOS or Android

On iOS go to your phone's wifi settings and connect your phone to the dride wifi network The default password is ilovedride

Android users will be connected automatically.


Once you’re connected to wifi, open the Dride app. The clips will begin to load on the screen.
